How it works

Edge‑ready. Shop‑floor proof.

Connect via OPC UA, learn on your data, and deploy models on the edge. Insights flow straight into your dashboards.

1

Connect

OPC UA to SINUMERIK / PLCs. Stream signals (torque, current, position, speed) at high frequency.

2

Learn

Clean, align, and label time‑series. Train robust models (ANN/LSTM) tailored to your process.

3

Predict

Run models on an edge box for low‑latency predictions. Monitor confidence and drift.

4

Act

Push alerts, suggest parameters, and close the loop to reduce scrap and increase OEE.
